Meet Laura

My fitness journey started with one simple belief: everyone deserves to feel powerful and confident in their own body. I'm not just a trainer handing you a plan — I'm your coach, your biggest supporter, and your guide every step of the way. Every workout I create is tailored with my experience and expertise to help you become the absolute best version of yourself.

With certifications in Personal Training, Nutrition Coaching, and Strength & Conditioning, I've helped clients from every background write their stories. Some want to shed a few kilos, others want to feel stronger, some focus on longevity, others train to age well, and some are preparing for a big day like a wedding. Each journey is different, but there's one thing we all share — the drive for real, lasting results.

Based in Greenwich, I design personalized programs that evolve with your journey. My philosophy is simple but powerful: lasting change happens when you commit to growth, embrace every challenge, and celebrate progress — not just in fitness, but in every area of your life.

This is more than training. It's transformation.
